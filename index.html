Hey, I'm Anushka

Be pure like light, be true like light. Be true to yourself. People often judge without knowing the full story, forming opinions based on assumptions. But you should remain true to who you are, letting your own light shine.As the Bhagavad Gita says, (Chapter 6, Verse 5) 


    "उद्धरेदात्मनाऽऽत्मानं नात्मानमवसादयेत्। 
     आत्मैव ह्यात्मनो बन्धुरात्मैव रिपुरात्मनः।।"

"One must elevate oneself by one’s own mind, not degrade oneself. The mind is a friend to the self, and the mind is also the enemy of the self."
Just as light does not change its path for obstacles, stay unwavering in you values and purpose. When you are genuine and authentic, you illuminate the world around you with clarity and truth. Let your light be your guide uplifting yourself and inspiring others to find their own.


Be the light in someone’s life!
